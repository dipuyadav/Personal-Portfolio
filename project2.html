Program
The basic human anthropometry starts from human dimensions to the area required for any human activity, that forces you to take care of the negative spaces being formed. The role is to avoid those spaces from becoming non-accesible. The main motif of this design studio was to design a space considering the specific age group by understanding their behaviour and activity pattern. The brief was to design a pre school that includes open play area, library, space for art and craft etc.

Leaving room / meeting room, Admin, Staffroom, Classrooms, Rest room, Canteen, Library / art- craft room
