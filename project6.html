Basic Design

Decoding the structural system
Program
The exercise started by finding a Dry Tree Branch to explore. Further the exercise was to tie threads from nodes to either branch ends or to other nodes forming triangles. The exercise continues by sketch branches them in detail. It also includes making Its surface development model with rendered views and add \horizontal planes to create spaces with consideration of human scale.
Aim
The exercise aims in developing the sense of handling any given form at a particular scale and making it comfortable for human use balancing its negatives and positives.
Requirement
Triangulation, Surface Development, Conceptualization, Visualisation
