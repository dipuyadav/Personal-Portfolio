Program
This studio focuses on understanding the human scale and proportions with the space, exploring the principles of conceptualization and space making. A volume of 3m x 3m x 3m was given to design a studio unit for ourselves with minimum requirements of bathing, sleeping and workspace. The aim was to develop the understanding of human scale and the space relative to it.
