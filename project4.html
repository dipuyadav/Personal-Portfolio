Program
The program was to document the karamsad heritage streets and it’s activity pattern which affects it’s architecture. Karamsad is home to a variety of well-knit collection of houses, from which one of the oldest cluster of dwellings, is called Bhaya ni Khadki. The Khadki positioned inside the area in such a way that its entrance is through main activity node, that links the street to the adjoining main street.
About Street
Measuring and documentation of the site has revealed many design principles which were reflective to the function of the space. Brick and lime was used as both structural and decorative purpose. Openings of the building were set in a specific proportion and had decorative elements of Indo Saracenic style around it. Along with the materials and function aspects the library was also climate responsive in every manner with proper layirng in roofing system.
Program
As pavagadh designated a world heritage site by UNESCO and also karamsad has house and memorial of sardar patel the first Deputy Prime Minister of India. karamsad has potanttial devlope as a Tourist center. The aim was to design a Tourist information center which responds to immediate context as well as context of karamsad with functionally and visually.
About Site
The site is located near karamsad bus stop nearer to baps mandir. Site has commercial building,enduaction institute, residential and savasadan in immediate context. On the north-west edge excellent view of karamsad housing which has ornamentation on facade.
